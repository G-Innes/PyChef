Title: Smoked Salmon Fettuccine
Ingredients:
- heavy cream: 16.0
- quality moist salmon: 1.0
- parmesan reggiano cheese: 4.0
- fettuccine noodles: 0.5
Steps:
- Step 1: Cook noodles according to package directions, drain and place in cold water bath.  Keep a pan of hot water ready to reheat noodles.
- Step 2: In a 12 skillet, melt butter and lightly saut garlic for a minute  do not brown.
- Step 3: Pour in cream and bring to a light boil, then lower heat and simmer rapidly to reduce the sauce by half, or until thick and creamy.  Stir in asparagus and pine nuts, then add chunked up salmon (bite sized pieces) and lightly stir around and add reheated/drained noodles.
- Step 4: Serve immediately on warmed plates.  Top each serving with fresh grated Parmesan Reggiano and fresh lemon juice to taste.

Title: Lemon Pasta Alfredo (Vegan)
Ingredients:
- vegan pasta: 1.0
- cashew cream: 2.0
- vegan parmesan cheese: 1.0
- granulated onion: 0.5
Steps:
- Step 1: Bring water to a boil, and add your pasta. Cook for 8-10 minutes, depending on the texture of pasta you like. When done, reserve 1 c. of pasta water in a bowl. Then drain pasta. Do not rinse
- Step 2: While pasta is cooking, add olive oil to a heated saucepan.
- Step 3: Add garlic and cooked until browned
- Step 4: Add frozen peas and lemon juice.
- Step 5: Saute until peas are tender.
- Step 6: Stir in cashew cream, cheese, lemon zest, granulated onion, oregano, thyme and crushed red pepper. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes
- Step 7: After 10 minutes, the sauce will become thick.
- Step 8: Remove saucepan from heat.
- Step 9: Add 1 c. of reserved pasta water to thin the sauce.
- Step 10: Add salt and pepper to taste
- Step 11: Pour pasta into sauce and toss until pasta is well-coasted.
- Step 12: Serve

Title: Lightened Ricotta Stuffed Shells With Italian Sausage Ragu
Ingredients:
- pasta shells: 12.0
- turkey sausage: 12.0
- onion: 1.0
- parmesan: 1.0
Steps:
- Step 1: Prepare shells according to package directions minus 2 minutes (the shells will finish in the sauce) and drain.
- Step 2: Lay the shells out on foil or a backing sheet so that they don't stick together. Discard any that are broken.
- Step 3: Meanwhile, in a sautee pan over medium-high heat, brown the sausage.
- Step 4: Drain any excess fat and add the onion. Continue to cook until the onions are translucent.
- Step 5: Add garlic and cook for an additional minute.
- Step 6: Add water, stewed tomatoes and tomato paste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
- Step 7: In a large bowl, combine ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella, 1/2 cup Parmesan, basil and egg whites.
- Step 8: Preheat oven to 350F.
- Step 9: Spay a 13 x 9-inch baking dish with non stick. Fill shells with about 1 tablespoon of the cheese mixture each and place in a tight single layer in the baking dish.
- Step 10: Pour the ragu over the shells and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Cover and bake until the sauce is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
- Step 11: Remove, uncover and let stand about 10 minutes.

Title: German Rhubarb Cake with Meringue
Ingredients:
- rhubarb: 600.0
- sugar: 2.0
- sugar: 130.0
- vanilla extract: 5.0
- salt: 0.125
- eggs: 2.0
- flour: 150.0
- roasted almonds: 50.0
- baking powder: 2.0
- egg whites: 3.0
- sugar: 150.0
- almonds: 12.0
Steps:
- Preheat the oven to 350F Convection. Grease a round 26 cm Spring pan (9 1/2 inch).
- Wash, dry and peel the rhubarb.
- Cut it in little pieces, mix with 2 tablespoon of sugar and let sit for at least 1/2 hour. It will extract a lot of water that needs to be drained. Pat rhubarb dry for further use.
- In a kitchen machine beat together butter, sugar and vanilla extract until the butter is fluffy and the sugar is dissolved. Put in the eggs, one at a time and mix well.
- In a separate bowl sift together flour, ground almonds, salt and baking powder, add slowly to the egg mixture. Dont over mix.
- Fill dough into the spring pan, top with dried rhubarb and bake for 25 min.
- In the mean time prepare the meringue/ baiser topping. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Slowly add the sugar until meringue is firm and shiny.
- Spread the meringue evenly over the rhubarb and decorate with almond slices. Return to the oven for another 15 min. Cover the cake with aluminum foil after 5 min. in case the meringue does turn too dark.
- Cool completely before removing the cake from the pan.

Title: Roasted Carrot Cream Pasta
Ingredients:
- carrots: 1.0
- garlic: 6.0
- heavy cream: 0.6666667
- parmesan cheese: 1.0
- pasta: 1.0
Steps:
- Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a large bowl toss carrot rounds and garlic with the Olive oil, using your hands to thoroughly coat the carrots and garlic with oil.Season well with salt, pepper, and thyme.
- Step 2: Bake in a 9x13 inch baking pan about 45-50 minutes until carrots are tender. Take the pan out halfway through the cooking time and toss.When carrots are tender, remove from oven. Cook the pasta in a generous amount of salted water to al dente according to package directions. While the pasta is cooking make the carrot cream.In a blender or food processor puree the carrots and garlic together with the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese until smooth. When the pasta is done cooking reserve a cup and a half of the pasta water.
- Step 3: Drain pasta and add to a large mixing bowl. Toss with carrot cream, adding pasta water a little at a time until you get a nice consistency and the pasta is evenly coated.
- Step 4: Serve with Parmesan Cheese and crusty bread/garlic bread.
